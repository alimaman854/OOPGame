OOP Game Layout

- 2 player game
	- one player is the user
	- second player is the computer

Player Class:
- Name
- HP (health points)
- Attack power
- EP (Elemental points)
- Elemental power
-Defense power

Fight Mechanics:
- Basic attack
- Elemental attack
- Defend
- Escape fight (random chance at successfully escaping the fight)

How to win:
- Player wins once the opposing player's health is 0
